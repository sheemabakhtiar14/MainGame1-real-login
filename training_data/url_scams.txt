URL PHISHING SCAM EXAMPLES FOR TRAINING

1. FAKE BANKING URLS
Malicious: https://bank-of-america.security-verify.com/login
Legitimate: https://bankofamerica.com/login
Red Flags: Subdomain scam, extra words in domain, different TLD

2. FAKE SOCIAL MEDIA URLS
Malicious: https://facebook-security.com/verify-account
Legitimate: https://facebook.com/login
Red Flags: Hyphenated fake domain, not the official domain

3. FAKE E-COMMERCE URLS
Malicious: https://amazon-prime.shipping-update.net/track
Legitimate: https://amazon.com/your-orders
Red Flags: Multiple hyphens, suspicious subdomain, wrong TLD

4. FAKE PAYMENT PROCESSOR URLS
Malicious: https://paypal-secure.verification-center.org/login
Legitimate: https://paypal.com/signin
Red Flags: Multiple subdomains, fake verification center, wrong domain

5. FAKE GOVERNMENT URLS
Malicious: https://irs-refund.gov-treasury.com/claim
Legitimate: https://irs.gov/refunds
Red Flags: Hyphenated government agency, fake .com instead of .gov

6. FAKE STREAMING SERVICE URLS
Malicious: https://netflix-billing.account-update.net/payment
Legitimate: https://netflix.com/youraccount
Red Flags: Fake billing subdomain, suspicious domain structure

7. FAKE TECH COMPANY URLS
Malicious: https://microsoft-support.windows-security.org/scan
Legitimate: https://support.microsoft.com
Red Flags: Hyphenated company name, fake support domain

8. FAKE CRYPTO EXCHANGE URLS
Malicious: https://coinbase-pro.trading-secure.com/wallet
Legitimate: https://coinbase.com or https://pro.coinbase.com
Red Flags: Fake subdomain, wrong main domain

9. FAKE DELIVERY SERVICE URLS
Malicious: https://fedex-tracking.package-delivery.net/status
Legitimate: https://fedex.com/tracking
Red Flags: Hyphenated service name, fake delivery domain

10. FAKE ANTIVIRUS URLS
Malicious: https://windows-defender.security-scan.org/cleanup
Legitimate: Windows Defender is built into Windows, no separate login site
Red Flags: Fake software domain, misleading security branding

TYPOSQUATTING EXAMPLES:
- googIe.com (capital i instead of lowercase L)
- arnazon.com (rn instead of m)
- paypaI.com (capital I instead of lowercase l)
- faceb00k.com (zeros instead of o's)
- microsooft.com (extra o)

URL SHORTENER RISKS:
- bit.ly/suspicious-link
- tinyurl.com/fake-bank
- t.co/malicious-redirect
- Short URLs hide the real destination

LEGITIMATE URL CHARACTERISTICS:
- Official company domains without hyphens or extra words
- HTTPS encryption (but note: scammers can also use HTTPS)
- Correct spelling of company names
- Proper domain structure (company.com, not company-fake.com)
- No suspicious subdomains
- Government sites use .gov domains
- Educational institutions use .edu domains
- URLs match the company's known official website
