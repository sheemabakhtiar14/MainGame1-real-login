=== WHAT MAKES THESE URLS LEGITIMATE ===
- Official domains from recognized organizations
- Proper HTTPS encryption
- Clear, logical URL structure
- No suspicious characters or substitutions
- Consistent with organization's known web presence
- Proper use of subdomains (like support., help., www.)
- Legitimate file paths and parameters
- No URL shorteners or redirects
- Domain names match the claimed organization
- No typos or character substitutions in domain names
- Appropriate top-level domains (.com, .gov, .edu, .org)
